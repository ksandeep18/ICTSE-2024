About the Conference

NIT Warangal

National Institute of Technology Warangal is one of the premier technical institutions in India, established in 1959. Known for excellence in engineering education and research.

Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at NIT Warangal has been at the forefront of research in thermal sciences, fluid mechanics, and sustainable energy systems.

Conference Goals

To bring together researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share cutting-edge research in thermal sciences and sustainable energy technologies.

Conference Overview

The International Conference on Thermal Sciences and Sustainable Energy (ICTSSE 2024) provides a platform for researchers, engineers, and industry professionals to present their latest findings and innovations in the field of thermal sciences and sustainable energy.

This conference covers a broad spectrum of topics including heat transfer, fluid dynamics, renewable energy systems, energy efficiency, and environmental sustainability. We invite original research papers, review articles, and case studies that contribute to the advancement of these fields.

Submission Guidelines

We invite high-quality research papers on thermal sciences and sustainable energy topics. All submissions will undergo rigorous peer review.

Paper Categories
  • Full Research Papers (6-8 pages)
  • Short Communications (3-4 pages)
  • Review Articles (8-10 pages)
  • Case Studies (4-6 pages)
Submission Requirements
  • Papers must be original and not published elsewhere
  • Follow IEEE conference paper format
  • Include abstract (200-250 words) and keywords
  • Submit through EasyChair platform

Author Instructions

Paper Preparation Guidelines

  • Use IEEE conference paper template
  • Page limit: 6-8 pages for full papers, 3-4 pages for short papers
  • Font: Times New Roman, 10pt
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • Single column format

  • Title and author information
  • Abstract (200-250 words)
  • Keywords (4-6 keywords)
  • Introduction
  • Literature Review
  • Methodology
  • Results and Discussion
  • Conclusions
  • References

  • Submit through EasyChair platform
  • Upload PDF format only
  • Include author bio and affiliation
  • Ensure paper is anonymous for review
  • Maximum file size: 10 MB

  • Double-blind peer review
  • Minimum 2 reviewers per paper
  • Review based on originality, technical quality, and clarity
  • Authors may be asked to revise and resubmit
  • Final decision within 6 weeks
